Tips for a successful audition

1. Learn your lines!

If your character seems angry don’t go yelling at your auditionees. Instead find an alternative approach to that anger try keeping it inside and show some facial expressions that the pot is about to blow.

2. Reverse the verse

Let it shine through. Don’t give one-word answers when having a conversation with the casting director. Ask questions!

3. React

Use your own internal monologue to play out your characters reactions. If your character is sad try pull on a sad thought, if happy a happy one.

4. Ask questions

Your auditions are not only about your performance it's a chance to pose questions to your auditionees about the project. Have some questions at the ready it’ll show great initiative.

5. Show passion

You must have passion to go through the auditioning process. We won’t lie to you it’s a long road and only a few are so lucky to land that big role first time round. So keep your passions alive and learn from your mistakes, use every audition as a tool to better then next one.
